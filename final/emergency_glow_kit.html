🌸Emergency Glow-Up Kit🌸

Welcome to your ultimate DIY guide for surviving the apocalypse in style—because just because the world’s ending doesn’t mean your glow has to. This project is all about creating your own Emergency Glow-Up Kit: a fierce fusion of beauty, badassery, and survival essentials. Whether you're dodging zombies, leading your girl gang, or just trying to feel human in the chaos, this kit will have you looking fresh, feeling confident, and staying ready for whatever comes next. Let’s prove that even in the darkest times, you can still shine.

🌸Step 1: Choose your Container🌸

Find something compact, durable, and cute.
Options: small ammo box, metal lunchbox, mini backpack, or a sturdy makeup bag
Bonus points if it’s waterproof or fire-resistant.

🌸Step 2: Pack the Skin Essentials🌸

Even end-times skin deserves TLC.
Include:
Tinted moisturizer with SPF
Facial cleansing wipes
Lip balm (tinted = glam + practical)
Hydrating mist or face spray

🌸Step 3: Add Multipurpose Makeup🌸

You want items that do double (or triple) duty.
Key picks:
Cream blush (can double as lip color and even eyeshadow)
Highlighter stick (for glow and emergency signaling)
Waterproof mascara (cry-proof, sweat-proof, apocalypse-proof)
Compact mirror (survival tool and brow check)

🌸Step 4: Don’t Forget Hair Control🌸

Whether you’re running from raiders or just trying to tame the frizz.
Dry shampoo
Hair ties / scrunchies
Headband or scarf (for flair and functionality)

🌸Step 5: Hygiene Heroes🌸

Stay fresh. Stay fierce.
Travel-size deodorant
Toothbrush + mini toothpaste
Hand sanitizer or cleansing gel
Feminine hygiene items (pads/tampons/cups)

🌸Step 7: Seal and Style It🌸

Time for the finishing touches
Zip it up, label it ("In Case of Apocalypse, Break Open"), and stash it in your bug-out bag, glove box, or under-bed stash. Add a charm or patch to make it yours.
