What is this Website About?

This site is about a fictional world where there is a Zombie Apocalypse, no Civilization, but also the internet!?! Its a blog about how to survive in such a strange world.

Who is this Website For?

This website is for anyone who finds the combination of these two things funny. In the fictional world created though, its a girls site for girls ONLY!

What inspired Me to Create this Website?

I was inspired by barbie, bratz, and resident evil. Growing up as a girl raised by my dad, it was sometimes difficult to express my love for girly things, as well as my shared passion for more "boyish" interests like zombies and survival horrpr. I think this website is a cute blend of both.
