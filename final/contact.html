Who I am, and why I created this page:

I grew up with a lot of these girly websites. My favorite site to visit when I was little was barbie.com. Now that site is just a place to sell the dolls, but when I was younger it had games and other activities for children. I always loved the messaging. You can be girly and strong at the same time. I wanted this website to give that same type of energy. Strength and resiliance can take many forms. You might not ever find yourself surviving a zombie apocalypse, but you can always dress confident and slay.


I've always felt like my love of girly barbies and bratz conflicted with my interest in zombies and that kind of media. I wanted to show they can come together and be the same thing. You don't have to be a big bulky strongman to take on difficult tasks. We all have our own way of adapting to things that are scary, and finding our own personal strengths. This is definetly an exaggeration of that, but I think its good to re-think what you might consider a strong person to look like. Maybe it is a body builder or a lumberjack or something, but it can also be a woman who is confident in herself, and unafraid to be who she is.
